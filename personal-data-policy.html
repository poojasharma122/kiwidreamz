Personal Data Policy

At Kiwi Dreamz, we value privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Personal Data Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and manage your data when you interact with our website or contact us directly

1. What We Collect

We only collect the data we need to provide a better experience. This includes:

  • Personal details: Name and email address (e.g. if you sign up for our newsletter or contact us)

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, and general location (collected via analytics tools)

  • Device information: IP address, screen size, and device type

  • Optional data: Hotel preferences or travel notes (only if voluntarily provided)

2. How We Use Your Data

We collect personal data for specific and limited purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries or support requests

  • To send newsletters or updates (if you subscribe)

  • To improve the website and its content

  • To ensure security and prevent misuse or fraud

  • To analyse website performance (via tools like Google Analytics)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard protection methods. Where applicable, we use secure third-party platforms (e.g. email list providers or analytics tools) with strong privacy safeguards in place.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the original purpose. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We only share your information when:

  • Required by law (e.g. court order or legal investigation)

  • It’s necessary for functionality (e.g. email platforms to send newsletters)

  • To maintain or troubleshoot the website securely (e.g. trusted hosting or tech providers)

All third-party providers we use meet standard data protection obligations.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information

  • Request deletion of your personal data

  • Withdraw your consent to receive marketing emails

  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@kiwidreamz.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Kiwi Dreamz uses cookies to:

  • Monitor website usage and improve performance

  • Track referral sources and user journeys

  • Support functionality like newsletter pop-ups or embedded maps

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work as intended.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or business operations. If changes are significant, we’ll note it clearly on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle personal data, contact us directly at:

Email: info@kiwidreamz.com
