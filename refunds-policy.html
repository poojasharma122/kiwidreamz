Refunds Policy

At Kiwi Dreamz, we are a content and listing platform designed to help users explore and compare hotel experiences across New Zealand. Please read this policy carefully to understand how refunds and disputes are handled in relation to our website and any third-party links or bookings you may engage with.

1. We Do Not Process Bookings

Kiwi Dreamz is not a hotel booking provider or travel agent. We do not take payments, make reservations, or manage transactions of any kind on behalf of users or hotels.

All bookings, payments, cancellations, and refunds are handled directly through the hotel or third-party platforms we may link to.

2. No Refunds for Website Use

Accessing or browsing our website is free. We do not charge users for viewing hotel listings, reading content, or signing up for newsletters. As such:

  • We do not issue refunds, because we do not charge for services.

  • If you’ve experienced a problem with a third-party booking (e.g. a hotel or external travel site), you must contact that provider directly.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Affiliate and Partner Links

In some cases, Kiwi Dreamz may include links to external hotel or travel booking platforms. If you follow one of these links and complete a transaction, that transaction is between you and the external provider.

We are not responsible for:

  • Payment processing or booking errors

  • Changes in hotel availability or pricing

  • Disputes over service delivery, cancellations, or refunds

Please review the individual refund and cancellation policy of the provider or hotel before making a reservation.

4. Issues with Hotel Stays

If you’ve encountered a problem with a hotel listed on Kiwi Dreamz — such as misleading details or out-of-date content — we want to hear about it. While we cannot process refunds, we can:

  • Review the listing for accuracy

  • Contact the provider if necessary

  • Remove hotels that do not meet our quality standards

Send any concerns to info@kiwidreamz.com.
